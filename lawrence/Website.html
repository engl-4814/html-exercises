The Uno Club at Virginia Tech

Welcome to the offical webpage of the Uno Club at Virginia Tech! We are an officaly registered orginization that operates on school grounds. We meet every Tuesday at 6:30 at the Johnson Student Center, loctaed on the Southwest end of campus.

A beginners guide to Uno Club:

  • Prior Uno experince is recommended but not required.
  • We don't ask for dues, ever! The only thing we ask if that you show up, have a good time, and treat your fellow Uno players with the same respect they treat you with. However, fincanes may be required for outside club activites, such a hikes, theatre trips, or eating out.
  • Join the Uno Club GroupMe to stay updated on changes in club location, special events, and crossovers with other clubs.
