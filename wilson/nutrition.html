Nutrition

According to Marriam-Webster the definition of Nutrition is "the act or process of nourishing or being nourished specifically : the sum of the processes by which an animal or plant takes in and utilizes food substances"

Food

Food is vital to help you reach your goals. A genral rule of thumb is everything we eat had calories. Calories are a measure of how much energy is in food. Empty calories are foods that have calories, but little nutritional value. These include solid fats and added sugars. According to My Plate Choose variety: The best meals have a balance of items from different food groups. Make half of your plate vegetables and fruits.Make at least half of your grain servings whole grains. Drink fat-free or low-fat (1%) milk and water instead of soda, sports drinks, and other sugary drinks. Avoid large portions.

Grains - breads, cereal, pasta, rice
Dairy - milk, cheese, yogurt
Fruits - apples, oranges, berries, grapes, bananas
Vegetables - broccoli, beans, spinach, carrots, peas
Protein - beef, chicken, pork, eggs, nuts, fish

What to do

A calorie deficit is where you should start. This means when a person consumes less calories than they burn. How to figure out where you are you should Multiply your activity level value with BMR, you will get your total daily energy expenditure (TDEE). Subtract the TDEE from the total number of calories you consume. If the above number is negative, you are in a calorie deficit. The most healthy amount of pounds you should lose a week is no more than two. You must still eat in order to stay alive and well, make sure to eat at least 1,600 calories a day.
