Workouts

According to Marriam-Webster the definition of workingout is "a practice or exercise to test or improve one's fitness for athletic competition, ability, or performance."

Splits

A split is a routine that you choose to follow to target a certain area on your body. You should have a minimum of once a week as a rest day. The split that I use is as follows.

Monday- Push day (chest and shoulders)
Tuesday- Leg day
Wednesday- Pull day (back and bicepts)
Thursday- Glute focused legs
Friday- Ab day
Saturday- Whole body
Sunday- Rest

Choosing a routine that works for you is tricky. It will take a lot of trial and error but in the end will be worth it. A general rule of thumb is to aim for 6-8 reps per set. If you are doing more reps per set you should up the weight to you are doing Progressive Overload. Defined as "a principle of resistance training exercise program design that typically relies on increasing load to increase neuromuscular demand to facilitate further adaptations." Aim for 3-5 sets for each workout.

What to do

I will typically do 4 sets at the maximum weight I can do for around 6 reps. For my 5th and final set I will drop the weight and do a drop set and push myself until failure on a light weight.

  1. For a push day you will be pushing weights away from your body. The workouts I do are: Inline press, Close Press, Regular Press, Shoulder Press, Kick Back, Skull Crushers, Straight bar pull down, Over head pull, Flys, Front Flys, Push Downs
  2. For leg day you are focusing on the lower half of your body. The workouts I do are: Abduction and Adduction Legs, Leg kick back, squats, calf raises, RDL, Hip Trust, Leg Curl, Leg Extention, Leg Press, Goblet Squat, Weighted Lunges, Bulgarian split squats.
  3. For pull day you will be pulling the weight to your body. The workouts I do are: Lat pull down, sitting rows, rope pull to face, cable curls, one arm pull on the ground using the cable, rows, standing rows, curls, hammer curls, shurgs, reverse flys.
  4. Glute focued leg day is the same as leg day but focused all on glutes. The workouts I do are: Crusty lunge squats, good mornings, hip trust pulse, cable kickback. slanted kickback, crossover kickback, drop lunge, RDL, Bulgarian split squats.
  5. Ab day is all about the middle of your body. The workouts I do are: Russian twist, planks, sit ups, crunches, mountain climbers, flutter kick, bicycle kicks, hollow hold.
  6. For a whole body day you will be using any of the listed items from the past days and hit them ever so slightly. You do not want to hit them too hard as you will be doing so many different exercises and hitting all aspects of your body.
  7. Rest day is one of the most important days. Your musscules need time to rest and recharge so you will be able to work them out again. You are intentinally shreding your musscules to build them up so therefore need time to get back together.
