English Department Poster

Kayla designed this poster as part of her curriculum for a class she took titled "Visual Rhetoric and Document Design" during the Spring of 2024. For the capstone class for Kayla's major in English Literature, she wrote a 16-page paper on the play titled "The Witch of Edmonton" for her final. The paper observes the play as a means of observing the dangers of social stasis and goes into special detail regarding the "Dog" character.
