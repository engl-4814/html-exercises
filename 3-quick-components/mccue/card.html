Favorite Albums of 2021

Sour - Olivia Rodrigo

When this album came out, I don't think there was one person who didn't listen to it. It took the world by storm and its staying power on the charts and Rodrigo's ability to keep getting awards for it even today prove that it was one of the best.

Happier Than Ever - Billie Eillish

While not as cohesive as her debut, HTE by Eillish showed off her range and proved that, at only 19, she is a force to be reckoned with.
