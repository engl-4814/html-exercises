Brunch Ideas for the Holidays

Discussing Holiday Brunch

Brunch is one of my favorite times to eat, especially when the weather is breezy and clear. Thus, I thought of creating a list of foods for brunch on holidays.

This website currently features brunch ideas for three holidays: Christmas, Thanksgiving, and Valentine's Day. Foods and their recipes wil be listed on each page, along with links and attribution to them.

I hope you have as much fun making brunch as much as I have fun eating it.

Other Brunch Recipes

For those who want to try out more broad brunch recipes, I have attached a few recipes here.

  • Crepes

    Crepes can be dressed up in many ways, allowing for a sweet or savory addition to the day.

    Lauren Allen posted a recipe on Tastes Better from Scratch for strawberry crepes. (They are my favorite kind of crepes!)

  • Mock mimosas

    I have never tried a mimosa before (I am under the legal drinking age in my country), but I do think a mimosa recipe just has to find its way on a webpage about brunch.

    Thus, I found a recipe by Lisa Longley on Simple Joy for mimosas without alcohol.
