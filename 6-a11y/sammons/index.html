My favorite things

Video Games

I love video games, there really is no other way to put it. Video games, in my opinion, are the best storytelling medium that we have. They are the perfect balance between immersion, deep storytelling, and interactvitiy. I have several thousand hours logged in various video games and for the most part i have loved every second of it. I think everyone should play video games in some capacity, they are the best way I have found to challenge myself and relax. Although I haven't been able to play very much recently, I hope to get back to playing more as some new stuff comes out this year.

Some of my absolute favroite games, in no particular order

  • Halo
  • Red Dead Redemption
  • Stardew Valley
  • Stellaris
  • Doom
  • Borderlands
  • The Witcher

Movies

My second favroite storytelling medium is movies. I love movies, they're not as interactive as video games, but sometimes that's exactly what you want out of it. Being able to just sit back and enjoy a movie is one of life's simple pleasures for me. I also love going to the theater to watch a movie, seeing it on the big screen has always been something that I love doing.

Some of my favroite movies, in no particular order

  • The Dark Knight
  • Star Wars
  • Pulp Fiction
  • Casablanca

Legos

I couldn't find an image that wasn't copyrighted in some way for the Lego I wanted to show, but my third favroite thing is Lego. I love building Lego, it is so much fun. I main focus on Star Wars sets, but I have a few others. Believe it or not, given the prices of both video games and movies nowadays, Lego is my most expensive hobby, and probably the one I get to do the least. Once I graduate and have adult money I intend to do a lot more Lego building than what I do now.
