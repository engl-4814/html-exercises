    discription of engineering majors

    • Electrical Engineering

      "The creativity of electrical engineers has revolutionized our world. They create the methods and devices to detect, gauge, transform, transmit, manage, and receive both energy and information. In their work, electrical engineers tackle projects ranging from tiny robots the size of mosquitoes to overseeing enormous structures spanning millions of miles, like the country's power network"

    • Civil Engineering

      "Civil engineers are the principal designers, constructors, operators, and maintainers of many of the constructed facilities in our society."

    • Chemical Engineering

      "Chemical engineers have contributed to advancements in atomic science, polymers, paper, dyes, medications, plastics, fertilizers, food products, petrochemicals, vaccines, novel approaches for carbon capture, and many other areas."

    • Areospace Engineering

      "The aerospace engineering program covers topics such as aerodynamics, flight dynamics and controls, propulsion, and aerospace structures. It concludes with a senior-level design sequence that is nationally recognized, focusing on the analysis and design of aircraft, spacecraft, and associated technologies."

    • Biomedical Engineering

      "Biomedical engineers concentrate on merging technology and medical science to create innovative devices and apparatus aimed at enhancing human health. They may, for instance, develop software to operate medical devices or use computer simulations to evaluate novel drug treatments."
